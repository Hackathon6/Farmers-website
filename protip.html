Coffee

About

Coffee was introduced in India near Chikmagalur in Karnataka. The most commonly used coffee beans are Arabica and Robusta grown in the hills of Karnataka (Kodagu, Chikkamagaluru and Hassan), Kerala (Malabar region) and Tamil Nadu (Nilgiris District, Yercaud and Kodaikanal). Now coffee is enjoyed all around the world as drinks, air freshners, fertilizers and more!

Conditions

Coffee trees are usually planted during the rainy season as it is easier to dig holes and the roots are able to spread through the moist soil easier. Other conditions are:-

  • planting their trees on east-facing slopes, where the sun only shines in the morning
  • ensuring their trees are well-watered
  • Suitable temperatures are from 15 degrees to 25 degrees Celcius
  • an annual rainfall of 59 to 118 inches (preferably on the lower end of this range)

Corn

About

Corn is second most plentiful cereal grown for human consumption . Corn is versatile crop and everything on a corn plant is useable. There are many various by-products such as corn starch, corn oil, corn flakes etc.

Conditions

  • It's usually sown just before monsoon starts
  • Temperature suited is 22-30 degrees Celcius
  • Corn can be affected by frost, hence it is grown when the coming months are frost free
  • Soil containing pH of 5.5 to 7.0 is prefered
  • Plowing should be done to get fine tilth, it may take 6 or 7 plows. At least 13 to 14 tons of Farm Yard Manure per hectare (F.M.Y) should be spread across the field or composted coir pith and apply 10 Azospirillum packets in the field. Prepare furrows and ridges with 45 cm to 50 cm spacing to save the irrigated water

Wheat

About

Wheat is one of the world's most commonly consumed cereal grains. Bread wheat, or common wheat, is the primary species. Wheat has high carbohydrates, proteins and fibre

Conditions

  • The wheat crop requires a well-pulverized but compact seed bed for good and uniform germination
  • Places with a cool, moist weather for most part of the year followed by a short, dry and warm spell is most suited for wheat cultivation
  • Wheat, in India, is best grown as a rabi or winter season crop since the conditions during that time are conducive for growth and ensures maximum yield. Since wheat is capable of tolerating severe cold it resumes growth in summer after being dormant in winter.

  • Major bread companies

Potato

About

Potatoes functions to provide food to the leafy to the leafy part of the plant . Potatoes are rich in fiber , potassium , vitamin C , vitamin B6. It's by products are Potato flakes , Potato fries, Potato hash etc.

Conditions

  • Potato plants will not begin to grow until the soil temperature has reached 7 degrees C
  • The soil should be moist, but not water-logged
  • A week or two before your planting date, set your seed potatoes in an area where they will be exposed to light and temperatures between 16-21 degrees F.

Rice

About

Rice is a staple food for two-thirds of the India's population . Rice is good source of complex carbohydrates . Rice is rich vitamin C , vitamin A , beta-carotene. It's by-products are Rice Vinegar Rice bread Rice Noodles

Conditions

  • Temperature: 20°C and 27°C
  • Rice cultivation is done only in those areas where minimum rainfall is 115 cm
  • Fertile riverine alluvial soil is best for rice cultivation.

Barley

About

Barley is the major cereal grown in temperature climates . It was one of the first cultivated grains . It is used in soups and stews . It's by products bread soups cereals

Conditions

It grows best in well-drained, fertile loams or light, clay soils in areas having cool, dry, mild winters. It also does well on light, droughty soils and tolerates somewhat alkaline soils better than other cereal crops. With many varieties of barley to choose from, be sure to select a regionally adapted one.

rajma

About

It's by product are rajma oil etc.

Conditions

For spring season, best time for Kidney beans cultivation is February-March and for Kharif season, it is sown during May-June month. In Punjab, some farmers sow Kidney beans in last week of January. It can be grown on wide range of soils from light sandy to heavy clay soils. Well drained loamy soil is good for kidney beans cultivation. It is very sensitive to saline soils.
